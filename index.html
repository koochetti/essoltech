Design(Modeling and Detailing)

  • Modeling and detailing are the representation of design in 3D and 2D format used for downstream manufacturing engineering to fabricate and manufacture the product. We at ESSOLTECH offer modeling and detailing as a part of product development, design upgrades or product refurbishment and conversion from 2D to 3D. By drawing upon our established expertise in this area, we offer:
    1. Parametric feature-based solid modeling for modular-based designs.
    2. Domain-specific modeling such as sheet metal, castings, plastics, structures, hydraulics, etc.
    3. Freeform surface modeling to develop an aesthetic class A surface to industry requirements.
    4. Modeling Techniques of Bottom Up design and Top down design based on the requirement.
    5. Digitization (Conversion of pdf to soft copy such as in 2D or 3D).
